The Best Movies According to Michael

My Top 3 Movies.

Inception (2010)

A mind-bending thriller by Christopher Nolan, starring Leonardo DiCaprio, Tom Hardy, and Cillian Murphy, renowned for its gripping story, stunning effects, and intense action.

12 Angry Men (1957)

A timeless classic, this black-and-white film follows one juror's mission to instill reasonable doubt in his peers, questioning the guilt of a young defendant.

Pulp Fiction (1994)

Quentin Tarantino's action-packed masterpiece featuring unforgettable dialogue and electrifying chemistry between Samuel L. Jackson and John Travolta. The restaurant scene is a must-watch.
